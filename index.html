About Me

I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at University College Dublin (UCD). My research focuses on computer vision and image processing, where I leverage statistical machine learning and deep learning techniques to address interdisciplinary challenges in multimedia computing.


I have strong expertise in designing, modeling, and implementing object detection systems, developing practical solutions for diverse industry applications. My work integrates advanced machine learning algorithms with computer vision techniques to enhance visual content analysis and manipulation. In particular, I focus on enabling accurate object detection, tracking, and integration within video frames for targeted and real-world applications.
